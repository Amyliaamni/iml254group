Fashion Tips

Know Your own body shape

Choose the clothes that flatter your body, such as the A-line dresses, which are mostly suitable for all types of body shapes, and also the high-waisted pants that can elongate your legs.

Using accessories wisely

Using accessories like belts or any jewelry that can match your outfit. You have to remember to use accessories less than more because to avoid overwhelming your look.

Wearing confidently

Always feel confident with your own outfit. Do not feel afraid of color, which is to add bright color that can make your outfit brighter and mix color or color-blocking to make a statement.
