BODY SHAPE

Apple Shape Body

The apple body shape is distinctive and has several key characteristics: Rounded Silhouette: This body shape is often recognized by a rounder silhouette, especially around the midsection. Broad Shoulders and Fuller Bust: Individuals tend to have broader shoulders and a fuller bust when compared to their hips.

Hourglass Shape Body

An hourglass figure is a body shape that is wider at the top and bottom, and narrower in the middle. It's characterized by a well-defined waist and bust and hips that are nearly the same width.

Rectangle

A rectangle body shape is characterized by a straight silhouette with similar measurements for the shoulders, waist, and hips. People with this body type may have minimal curves and a lean build, but it can occur in any height and size.

Pear

A pear-shaped body typically has narrower shoulders and a smaller bust compared to the hips and thighs, creating a silhouette resembling a pear. The main physical characteristics include a defined waistline, and fuller hips, thighs and buttocks.

Triangle

A triangle body shape, also known as a pear shape, is a body type where the shoulders and bust are narrower than the hips. People with this body shape typically have a defined waist and slim arms.
